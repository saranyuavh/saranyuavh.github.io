Welcome to Yoda Books!

In Yoda Books, you can search in a whole library of books, add the books to your favourites or cart, rent books. You can add books directly to your favourites list or cart, add favourites to your cart, ca rent books directly from your cart. You can monitor your rent history, rents in process in rental history tab. It's a simple, hassle free and highly useful platform for you bibliophiles!
